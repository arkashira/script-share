# Business Model Canvas for Script-Share

## Customer Segments
- Mechanical engineers and scientists who require Python for scientific programming.
- Non-technical users looking for a user-friendly platform to execute scripts.
- Educational institutions teaching Python programming to engineering and science students.

## Value Propositions
- User-friendly interface enabling non-technical users to set up and execute Python scripts easily.
- Collaborative features allowing users to share scripts and work together in real-time.
- Access to a library of pre-built scripts tailored for engineering and scientific applications.

## Channels
- Online platform accessible via web browsers.
- Marketing through engineering and scientific forums, social media, and educational institutions.
- Partnerships with universities for integration into their curriculum.

## Customer Relationships
- Community support through forums and user groups for troubleshooting and sharing best practices.
- Regular webinars and tutorials to educate users on maximizing the platform's features.
- Feedback loops for continuous improvement based on user input.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: $10/month (approximately 350 THB) for individual users.
- Institutional licenses for universities and companies, starting at $500/year (approximately 17,500 THB).
- Premium features and add-ons, priced at $5-$20 per feature (approximately 175-700 THB).

## Key Resources
- Development team for ongoing platform enhancements and maintenance.
- Marketing team to drive user acquisition and engagement.
- Partnerships with educational institutions for credibility and reach.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development of the platform to enhance user experience and add features.
- Marketing campaigns targeting mechanical engineers, scientists, and educational institutions.
- Community engagement through forums, webinars, and user feedback sessions.

## Key Partners
- Educational institutions for curriculum integration and user base expansion.
- Python programming communities and forums for marketing and user acquisition.
- Technology partners for cloud hosting and data management solutions.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs: Estimated at $100,000/year (approximately 3,500,000 THB) for a small team.
- Marketing expenses: $20,000/year (approximately 700,000 THB) for campaigns and promotions.
- Operational costs: $10,000/year (approximately 350,000 THB) for hosting and maintenance.
