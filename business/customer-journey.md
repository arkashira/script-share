```markdown
# Customer Journey Map for Script-Share

## Phase 1: Aware
- **Trigger Event:** Mechanical engineers and scientists encounter challenges with existing Python tools that require extensive coding knowledge.
- **Friction Points:** Lack of awareness about user-friendly alternatives; existing tools feel intimidating.
- **User Emotions:** Frustration, confusion, curiosity.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Create engaging content (tutorials, webinars) showcasing ease of use; leverage testimonials from early adopters.
- **Metric:** Number of website visits and engagement with awareness content.

---

## Phase 2: Consider
- **Trigger Event:** Users research solutions to simplify their Python scripting needs.
- **Friction Points:** Overwhelmed by options; skepticism about the platform's capabilities; concerns about learning curve.
- **User Emotions:** Hopeful, cautious, analytical.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Provide clear comparisons with existing tools; offer a free demo or interactive walkthrough.
- **Metric:** Number of demo requests and time spent on comparison pages.

---

## Phase 3: Try
- **Trigger Event:** Users sign up for a trial or demo of Script-Share.
- **Friction Points:** Initial setup complexity; potential technical issues; lack of immediate support.
- **User Emotions:** Excitement mixed with anxiety; eagerness to see results.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Offer personalized onboarding support; provide quick-start guides and responsive customer service.
- **Metric:** Trial conversion rate and user engagement during the trial period.

---

## Phase 4: Adopt
- **Trigger Event:** Users successfully complete their first project using Script-Share.
- **Friction Points:** Transition from trial to paid version; concerns about ongoing support and updates.
- **User Emotions:** Satisfaction, relief, confidence.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Celebrate user milestones; offer loyalty discounts or referral bonuses; ensure seamless transition to paid plans.
- **Metric:** Conversion rate from trial to paid subscriptions.

---

## Phase 5: Expand
- **Trigger Event:** Users begin to explore advanced features or recommend Script-Share to colleagues.
- **Friction Points:** Limited awareness of all available features; potential for feature overload.
- **User Emotions:** Enthusiasm, pride, loyalty.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Regularly update users on new features; create a community forum for sharing tips and scripts; host user feedback sessions.
- **Metric:** User retention rate and number of referrals generated.
```
